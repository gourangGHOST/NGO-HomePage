“There are people in the world so hungry, that God cannot appear to them except in the form of bread.” ~ Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi.

“Aadi foundation” is a delhi based NGO. It’s headquarter is in Rohini, Sector-16. Aadi foundation is a not-for-profit, non-governmental organization that aims to work for the upliftment and betterment of society. We work on creating awareness about environmental and social issues through educational seminars and interactive classroom sessions. Our focus is on creating awareness among the youth and the younger generation. ...

The establishment has a three-point programme which is
1. Hunger eradication
2. Education
3. Sanitation

Our simple message is that everyone should contribute to make this world a better place and this is the only and only way to make it happen. Donate whatever in whatever amount it does not matter what matters is your contribution and this contributions will blooms into flowers like harmony, peace and satisfaction in return thus making your own life peaceful. It is the matter of simple transaction, you can donate, be it money or if you cannot help us prepare our programmes or volunteer for community service. Now again there is not any hard and fast rule that you should volunteer with us only, join any other NGO’s near you. The crux of the matter is to ‘Help’.
